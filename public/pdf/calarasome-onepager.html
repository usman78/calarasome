Core workflows
Payments

Deposit handling that protects revenue

Eligible near-term bookings can use authorization holds instead of immediate charges, giving clinics policy enforcement without unnecessary refund cleanup when patients cancel on time.

Waitlist

Structured waitlist recovery

When a slot opens, the system helps your team offer it to the best-fit waitlist patients first, instead of restarting outreach from scratch.

Insurance

Insurance follow-up before the visit

Insurance-heavy bookings can be routed into an admin queue so your team sees what needs attention before the patient arrives, not at the front desk.

Privacy

Communication matched to consent

Patients choose how they want to receive communication. Private flows keep sensitive details out of email and move patients into a secure verification page instead.
